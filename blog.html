Introducing Primary Students to the World of Coding with Scratch

This week, our young learners took their first steps into the world of programming through Scratch. Scratch is a visual programming language developed by MIT that makes it fun and easy for kids to create their own interactive stories, games, and animations. Our students learned to think creatively, work collaboratively, and reason systematically. The highlight was seeing the students bring their imaginations to life, designing characters and directing them through mazes of their own creation. Their excitement was palpable as they shared their projects with the class.
